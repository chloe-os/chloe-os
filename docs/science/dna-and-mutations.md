# DNA & Mutations

## What Is DNA?

Every cell in your pet's body contains DNA — a long molecule that acts like an instruction manual for building and running the body. You can think of DNA as a very long string of letters written in a four-letter alphabet: A, T, G, and C.

These letters spell out instructions called **genes**. Genes tell cells how to make **proteins** — the molecular machines that do almost everything in the body.

## What Are Mutations?

A mutation is a change in the DNA sequence — like a typo in the instruction manual. Most mutations are harmless. Some are even beneficial. But when certain mutations accumulate in specific genes, they can cause cells to grow and divide uncontrollably. That's cancer.

## How Cancer Happens

Normal cells follow rules: they grow, divide, and die in an orderly way. Cancer starts when mutations break these rules:

1. **Growth signals** — Mutations can make a cell produce constant "grow" signals
2. **Brake failure** — Mutations can disable the cell's "stop growing" controls
3. **Immortality** — Normal cells have a limited lifespan; cancer cells can divide indefinitely

It usually takes multiple mutations for cancer to develop, which is why cancer is more common in older animals.

## Tumor vs. Normal DNA

Your pet's healthy cells all carry the same DNA (with minor variations). The tumor's DNA started the same way but has accumulated additional mutations. By comparing tumor DNA to normal DNA, we can find the mutations specific to the cancer — these are called **somatic mutations**.

These somatic mutations are what Chloe OS analyzes. They're unique to the tumor, which makes them potential targets for the immune system.

## What Is a VCF File?

A VCF (Variant Call Format) file is the standard way to list DNA differences between samples. When a sequencing lab compares your pet's tumor DNA to normal DNA, the differences are recorded in a VCF file. Each line represents one mutation — where it is, what the original letter was, and what it changed to.

This is the file you give to Chloe OS to start the analysis.
