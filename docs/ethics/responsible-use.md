# Responsible Use

Chloe OS puts powerful tools in the hands of pet owners. With that power comes responsibility.

## Do

- **Involve professionals** at every stage
- **Manage expectations** — understand that results are predictions, not guarantees
- **Continue proven treatments** as recommended by your veterinarian
- **Share knowledge** — contribute to the community dataset to help other pets
- **Report issues** — if you find bugs or concerns, let us know

## Don't

- **Don't skip the vet** — No amount of computational analysis replaces clinical expertise
- **Don't manufacture vaccines at home** — mRNA synthesis requires specialized equipment and expertise
- **Don't delay treatment** — Standard-of-care treatment should not be postponed for experimental approaches
- **Don't over-interpret results** — A high-scoring neoantigen is a candidate, not a cure
- **Don't sell or commercialize results** — This tool is for research and personal use

## For Researchers

If using Chloe OS in a research context:

- Validate predictions against experimental data before drawing conclusions
- Cite the specific tool versions and parameters used
- Follow your institution's animal research ethics guidelines
- Consider contributing improvements back to the project

## Reporting Concerns

If you become aware of misuse of Chloe OS — such as unqualified individuals administering untested treatments — please:

1. Open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/chloe-os/chloe-os/issues)
2. Contact the maintainers directly
3. Report to relevant veterinary regulatory authorities if animal welfare is at risk
