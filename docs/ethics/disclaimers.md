# Disclaimers

!!! danger "Not Medical Advice"
    Chloe OS is a **research tool**. It does not provide veterinary medical advice, diagnose disease, or prescribe treatment.

## What Chloe OS Is

- An open-source bioinformatics pipeline
- A tool that processes genomic data through established algorithms
- A way to make complex science more accessible
- A starting point for conversations with qualified professionals

## What Chloe OS Is Not

- A substitute for veterinary care
- A validated clinical diagnostic tool
- A guarantee that identified neoantigens will work as vaccine targets
- A replacement for standard cancer treatments

## Specific Limitations

1. **Prediction accuracy** — MHC binding predictions have significant false positive and false negative rates. A predicted binder may not actually trigger an immune response.

2. **Canine DLA validation** — MHC binding tools were primarily trained on human data. While some support canine DLA alleles, predictions for dogs are less validated than for humans.

3. **Single-tool analysis** — Chloe OS is one analytical approach. Clinical decisions should consider multiple lines of evidence.

4. **No clinical trials** — The pipeline as a whole has not been validated in controlled clinical studies.

5. **mRNA construct designs** — Any proposed mRNA constructs are theoretical starting points, not ready-to-use therapeutics.

## When Using Results

- **Always** share results with a qualified veterinarian
- **Never** use results as the sole basis for treatment decisions
- **Remember** that personalized cancer vaccines are experimental
- **Continue** standard-of-care treatment as recommended by your vet
